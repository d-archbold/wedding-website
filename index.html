Questions & Answers

Dress Code

The dress code is formal wear. We encourage you to wear colourful clothing but to be comfortable for warm weather. Please do not wear all white or black.

What is a Céilí?

A traditional Irish gathering featuring folk music and lively group dancing. It will be led by a "caller" who guides participants through the steps. You will dance most dances with a partner but don't worry about securing these in advance. Please wear sensible footwear for this portion of the evening.
