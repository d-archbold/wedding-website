Information

The Venue

The museum is open to the public until 5pm. A lift is available to access the reception rooms (2nd floor). We encourage you to explore the galleries; guest entry is included. Please keep drinks on the second floor only.

Dress Code

Cocktail. Colourful clothing is encouraged; please avoid all white or all black.

Céilí

A Céilí is an Irish dance. There will be a "caller" to teach the steps. We recommend bringing sensible footwear (flats) for this part of the evening!

Gifts

Your presence is the greatest gift. If you wish to contribute further, there will be a "cagnotte de mariage".

Parking

Free parking is available on-site at the Bowes Museum.
