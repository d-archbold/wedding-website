The Venue

The museum is open to the public until 5:30 PM. Please head to the second floor (Music Room) on arrival. A lift is available. Feel free to explore the galleries, but please keep drinks on the second floor only. Red wine will be served from the evening onwards.

Dress Code

Formal wear. Colourful clothing is encouraged; please avoid all white or all black.

What is a Céilí?

A traditional Irish gathering with group dancing. No experience needed! We recommend sensible footwear for dancing.
