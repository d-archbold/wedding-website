Questions & Answers

The Venue

The museum will be open to the public during the day until 5:30, please be respectful of the museum visitors. When you arrive please head to the second floor to the reception area (the music room). There is a lift available. During the day you're welcome to wander around the museum and view the artwork. Please note you cannot take your drinks around the galleries on the first or ground floor, you're ok to walk around the second floor with your drinks. Please note, red wine will not be served until the evening, to protect the floors.

Dress Code

Formal wear. Colourful clothing encouraged; please avoid all white or black.

What is a Céilí?

A traditional Irish gathering with group dancing. No experience needed! Please wear sensible footwear.

Gifting

Should you wish to give a gift, a wedding fund will be available on the day.

Parking

Free parking is available behind the museum in a dedicated car park.

Additional information about the day:

  • We will both be keeping our respective family names.
  • While we love your little ones, our wedding will be a child-free event (with the exception of Rhys's nephew Arthur).
  • We invite you to be fully present during our unplugged ceremony; please keep devices switched off until the reception.
