Questions & Answers

The Venue

The museum will be open to the public during the day until 5:30. When you arrive please head to the second floor to the reception area (the music room). There is a lift available.

Dress Code

Formal wear. Colourful clothing encouraged; please avoid all white or black.

What is a Céilí?

A traditional Irish gathering with group dancing. No experience needed! Please wear sensible footwear.

Gifting

Should you wish to give a gift, a wedding fund will be available on the day.

Parking

Free parking is available behind the museum in a dedicated car park.
